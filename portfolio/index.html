About Me

Hello! My name is Daniel Ward.

I am a web developer who has fullstack skills and is passionate about UI/UX design. I pride myself in writing clean,reusable and functional code. I began working with web design as a hobby; playing with applications on a Raspberry Pi using Python.

I have been developing programs/ websites for 2 years now and recently graduated from a FullStack bootcamp at UCSD. In bootcamp, I honed skills in backend development and learned how to integrate that with the frontend. I quite enjoy the problem solving nature of development and savoring 'victory moments' when all the pieces come together and the product works. I currently am doing freelance work and am interested in attaining a career working with a team on web application projets

When not working on projects, I love to learn new frameworks and languages to keep myself up to date with the everchanging web dev environment. In my free time I enjoy swimming, surfing, and spending time with my family.
